A Systematic Approach

The Curious Beginner's Guide To Effective Bible Study

A Note from
the Creator

To be honest, I originally created this for myself because I was struggling with Bible study. I kept searching for ways to study the Bible effectively, praying, pushing and trying different approaches, until one day, to God be the glory, my life changed.

In sharing this, I see it as an act of love. The Bible tells us in Gospel of John that just as Christ has loved us, we should love others freely as well. I know this can be a beautiful gift to anyone struggling with Bible study, and I hope your life changes too as you begin to approach Scripture in a systematic yet spiritually led manner.

My prayer is that the Holy Spirit will speak to you through the text, so that your study is not merely scholarly, but filled with genuine encounters with God.

♥

The Heart of Study

Before opening a tool or a commentary, we must prepare our hearts.

01

Reverence & Expectation

Approach the Bible with godly reverence, humility, curiosity, and the expectation that it will open up for you.

02

Spirit-Led Navigation

Pray and allow the Holy Spirit to guide and teach you. Sit still and allow Him to speak through the text.

03

Ultimate Transformation

Do not just master the text; let the text master you. The goal is to be transformed into the image of Christ.
