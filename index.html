A Note from
the Creator

To be honest, I originally created this for myself because I wanted more from the Bible. I wanted to learn how to uncover the wonderful mysteries of Scripture myself. So I kept pushing, praying, and trying different approaches until earlier this year, to God be the glory, my life changed.

In sharing this, I see it as an act of love. The Bible tells us in Gospel of John that just as Christ has loved us, we should love others freely as well. I know this can be a beautiful gift for anyone who wants to learn how to study the Bible effectively, and I hope your life changes too as you begin to approach Scripture in a systematic yet spiritually led manner.

My prayer is that the Holy Spirit will speak to you through the text, so that your study is not merely scholarly, but filled with genuine encounters with God.
